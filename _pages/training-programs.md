### NSF REU Site in Cryptography and Coding Theory

I direct an NSF-funded **Research Experiences for Undergraduates (REU) Site** in Cryptography
and Coding Theory at the University of South Florida. The program provides intensive summer
research experiences for undergraduate students, with close faculty mentoring and an
emphasis on mathematical foundations of modern cryptography.

The REU aims to broaden participation in mathematical research and to prepare students for
graduate study and research careers.

### NSF Research Training Group (RTG) in Applied Algebra

I am the Director of an NSF **Research Training Group (RTG)** in Applied Algebra at the
University of South Florida. The RTG supports training activities across multiple career
stages, including undergraduate students, graduate students, and postdoctoral researchers.

The program emphasizes research in cryptography, coding theory, and quantum computing,
and fosters collaboration, professional development, and interdisciplinary training.
